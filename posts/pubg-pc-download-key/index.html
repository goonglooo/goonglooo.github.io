Step 1: Downloading Steam#

  1. To begin, open up your browser and search for “Steam.” Click on the first link that appears, which will take you to the official Steam website.

  4. Once the installation is complete, click on “Finish” to launch the Steam application.

Step 2: Downloading the Main PUBG Variant#

  1. After opening Steam, create a free account if you don’t have one already, or sign in using your existing account.
  2. Go to the “Store” section within Steam and search for “PUBG” in the search bar.
  3. Click on the PUBG Battlegrounds game from the search results.
  4. Scroll down on the game page and click on the “Play Game” button.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions and click on “Next” to start the download and installation process.
  6. Once the game is downloaded and installed, go to the “Library” section within Steam and click on “PUBG Battlegrounds.” This will launch the game, and you can start playing.

Key Points:#
