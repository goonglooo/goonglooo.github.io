Tiantian 64 Bit Android Emulator OpenGL Settings Tutorial - Step by Step Guide to Fix Tiantian Graphics Bugs

August 13, 2023 · 3 min · 528 words · Elisa Lopez

Introduction

In this tutorial, we will be addressing the common issue faced by many users while running Fortnite on the Tiantian emulator. Specifically, we will focus on fixing the OpenGL settings to resolve graphics bugs. We will provide a step-by-step guide to help you fix the issue and optimize your gaming experience on the Tiantian emulator.

Step 1: Installing the Tiantian Emulator

To begin, make sure you have the Tiantian emulator installed on your desktop. There are two versions available - the normal vanilla version and the multi-version. In this tutorial, we will be using the multi-version of the Tiantian emulator.

Step 2: Accessing the OpenGL Settings

After installing the emulator, locate the mouse icon with several lines and click on it. This will open up the settings menu. Now, click on the cog icon on the right-hand side to access the advanced settings. Here, you will see various options for customization.

Step 3: Adjusting the Graphics Settings

First, we will adjust the resolution settings. Choose the desired resolution, such as 1280 x 720, from the available options. Next, click on the second option, which might be displayed in Chinese. In this option, set the RAM to 9090-9999 MB and choose 8 cores for the CPU if you have a multi-threading processor. Additionally, set the OpenGL option to enable it.

Step 4: Saving the Settings

After adjusting the settings, click on the save button to confirm and save the changes. This will ensure that the optimized graphics settings are applied when you launch the emulator.

Step 5: Launching the Tiantian Emulator

Now, open the Tiantian emulator and allow it some time to load. Once opened, you will notice the loading screen, indicating that the emulator is functioning properly. You can also test this by accessing the browser within the emulator and confirming that it is working.

Step 6: Troubleshooting Graphics Issues with Nvidia Control Panel (Optional)

If you still encounter graphics issues or bugs despite adjusting the OpenGL settings, follow this additional step. Right-click on your desktop and select the Nvidia Control Panel. In the global settings, make sure the OpenGL rendering GPU is set to your Nvidia GPU instead of auto-select. Under program settings, select the specific application, such as Command & Conquer or TM 10-q virtual box. Choose your Nvidia GPU and adjust the texture filtering quality as desired.

Conclusion and Seeking Help

By following these steps, you should be able to resolve the graphics bugs and optimize your gameplay experience on the Tiantian emulator.
